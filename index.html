faq

sharp answers before you ask

No growth-hacker fog machine. No secret bot stack. Just the uncomfortable details people usually leave out.

Are these actually real people, or bots with profile pictures?

Real people. Every account we grow belongs to an active, US-based profile with a genuine history, not a freshly-created shell built to look real for a week. No automation touches your order, and no fake engagement is ever used.

Will this get my account flagged or banned?

Fameshop avoids the behavior that gets accounts into trouble: bot delivery, mass fake actions, and click-farm sourcing. We never ask for your password or touch your account directly, only your public profile receives real activity, the same way any organic action would.

What does "USA-only" actually mean for my growth?

Every boost we deliver comes from a real, US-based account. No overseas click farms, no accounts that disappear the moment a platform sweeps for fake activity. It is the reason our growth survives those sweeps.

Do you ever need my password?

Never. We only need your public username or profile link. If anyone ever asks you for a password to grow your account, that is not us, and it should not be anyone.

What happens if growth drops after delivery?

Every order is covered by a 30-day refill window. If your count drops below what was delivered, we top it back up at no charge. No hostage pricing, no awkward retention dance.

How fast does delivery actually start?

Most orders start within hours of checkout. You will see movement on your profile the same day, not the same month, and you can watch the order log the whole time.

Can I preview before I pay?

Yes. Drop your handle and you will see a real before-and-after preview built from your own profile, before you ever reach checkout. No blind purchase.

Is the average growth multiplier guaranteed?

The promise is simple: if delivery doesn't produce real growth, you get your money back. Results depend on your niche, starting point, and how alive your platform mix is. Anyone promising the exact same outcome for every account is either lying or selling traffic that should not touch your account.

Why not guarantee an exact number every time?

Because real growth has gravity. A fashion page and a quiet local business account will not move the same way. Fameshop optimizes for accounts that will actually engage, not a vanity number padded with profiles that will never watch, like, or remember you.

What counts as "real" growth?

An active US-based account with a genuine history: a real photo, a real bio, and activity that predates your order. Not a giveaway account, not a bot-looking account, not a blank profile from nowhere.

Do you offer other engagement boosts too?

Yes. The same USA-only, manually verified approach applies across engagement types, attached to a specific public post URL you choose, with a preview before checkout.

Do you ever remove growth?

Only as part of routine account hygiene: inactive or dead accounts get quietly swapped out during a refill, never a churn tactic. If a delivered account goes inactive, replacing it is part of the guarantee, not an extra charge.

How much do I need to be involved?

Almost none. Drop your handle, choose a package, and check your email for the delivery report. That is the whole loop.

What do I get in the delivery report?

A plain breakdown of what happened: accounts screened, boost delivered, verification checks passed, and anything flagged and replaced along the way. No dashboard theater, no chart pretending "impressions" paid for anything.

Why not just use a $5 panel?

Because a $5 panel is a $5 panel: overseas click farms, accounts that vanish inside a month, and zero recourse when it happens. The price difference is the verification you are actually paying for.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, anytime. If delivery misses the guarantee, you get your money back. If the fit is wrong, the honest answer is to stop, not to bury you in "just one more month" energy.
